## FAQ

**Is this just keto?**
Keto is a tool. This book teaches you how to use that tool specifically to fix insulin resistance. It's not "eat bacon forever"—it's a structured approach with clear phases and an exit strategy.

**Do I need to do this forever?**
No. Power Keto is a reset protocol. Once your insulin sensitivity is restored, you can reintroduce carbs strategically. The book shows you how.

**Will this work if I'm diabetic?**
This book is educational, not medical advice. If you have diabetes, work with your doctor. That said, many diabetic men have used these principles successfully—under proper supervision.

**What if I have a lot of weight to lose?**
This approach works especially well for men with significant weight to lose, because insulin resistance is usually a bigger factor in those cases.
