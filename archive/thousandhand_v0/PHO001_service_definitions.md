# PHO Service Offerings
## Man vs Health - Personal Health Optimization Services

---

## OVERVIEW

PHO (Personal Health Optimization) services are the human-delivered coaching layer of Man vs Health. These services bridge the gap between self-guided resources (Man Plan, book) and full medical intervention.

**Position:** Premium support for men who want guidance, accountability, and a faster path to results.

---

## SERVICE TIER: JUMPSTART SESSION

### What It Is
A one-time deep-dive session where we review your current situation, identify your biggest metabolic obstacles, and build a personalized action plan.

### Duration
60-90 minutes (video call)

### What's Included
- Pre-session intake form (health history, current habits, goals)
- Review of any existing labs (if available)
- Assessment of insulin resistance risk factors
- Personalized Power Keto implementation plan
- Supplement recommendations (if applicable)
- 30-day action checklist
- Session recording for your reference
- One follow-up email for questions

### Who It's For
- Men just starting their metabolic health journey
- Men who've read the book but need help applying it
- Men who want a clear plan before committing to ongoing coaching

### Outcome
Leave with a concrete, actionable plan tailored to your specific situation—not generic advice.

### Price Recommendation
$297 (one-time)

---

## SERVICE TIER: ACCOUNTABILITY COACHING

### What It Is
Ongoing 1-on-1 coaching with regular check-ins to keep you on track, troubleshoot obstacles, and adjust your plan as you progress.

### Structure Options

**Option A: Weekly Check-ins**
- 4 x 30-minute video calls per month
- Async messaging support between calls
- Monthly plan adjustments

**Option B: Bi-weekly Check-ins**
- 2 x 45-minute video calls per month
- Async messaging support between calls
- Bi-weekly plan adjustments

### What's Included (Both Options)
- JumpStart session included in first month (or credited if already completed)
- Progress tracking dashboard
- Lab review when available
- Nutrition plan adjustments
- Exercise guidance integration
- Escalation to medical services if needed
- Direct access to Paul via messaging (response within 24-48 hours)

### Who It's For
- Men who need external accountability to stay consistent
- Men with complex situations requiring ongoing adjustment
- Men who value having a coach in their corner

### Minimum Commitment
3 months recommended (month-to-month available after)

### Price Recommendations
- Weekly: $397/month
- Bi-weekly: $247/month

---

## SERVICE TIER: INTENSIVE RESET

### What It Is
A concentrated 6-week program with intensive support through the hardest part of metabolic reset—the first weeks of Power Keto adaptation.

### Structure
- Week 0: JumpStart session + full intake
- Weeks 1-2: 3x weekly check-ins (daily if needed)
- Weeks 3-4: 2x weekly check-ins
- Weeks 5-6: Weekly check-ins + transition planning
- Async support throughout

### What's Included
- All JumpStart deliverables
- Meal planning support during adaptation
- Daily accountability during critical first two weeks
- Troubleshooting for keto flu, cravings, energy dips
- Transition plan to sustainable eating
- Option to continue with Accountability Coaching after

### Who It's For
- Men who've struggled with starting keto before
- Men who want maximum support during the hardest phase
- Men with busy lives who can't afford to figure it out alone

### Outcome
By week 6, you're fat-adapted, past the hardest part, and have a clear path forward.

### Price Recommendation
$997 (one-time, 6-week program)

- Triggered by: engagement with Man Plan, reading book, watching content

### From JumpStart to Accountability
- Triggered by: JumpStart completion

- Triggered by: lab results that need physician review, medication interest (TRT, GLP-1)

### From Accountability to Intensive
- Triggered by: struggling with consistency, repeated restarts
